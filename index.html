What is good design?

A good digital design should be both functional and aesthetically pleasing. It needs to effectively communicate the intended message while being easy to navigate and use. Simplicity is key—users should intuitively understand how to interact with the design without feeling overwhelmed. Good use of color, typography, and spacing is important to guide the user's attention. Consistency across elements helps build trust, and responsiveness ensures the design works well on different devices.
